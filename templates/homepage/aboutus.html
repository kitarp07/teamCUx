Who We Are?

TestMyUX is an online platform that allows website owners to test their websites before, during or after they have launched it. This platform focuses on connecting website owners with people who are qualified enough to review a website and think their thoughts out loud while they browse the site.

What We Do?

We, as customers, regularly see websites of giant companies like Google, Facebook and more, misfunctioning or containing broken links. With hundreds of qualified people who can review your website, go through custom tasks provided by you and check for bugs, you save yourself hundreds and thousands of dollars that you would else have to either lose or pay a bug bounty specialist to take care of.
